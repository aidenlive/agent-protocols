## Before contacting support

- Check the [documentation]({docs URL}).
- Search existing issues.
- For bugs, capture: version, environment, steps to reproduce, expected vs. actual.

## What we support

- Latest stable release
- Previous major version, for `{N months}` after a new major

## What we don't support

- Forks and modified builds
- End-of-life versions (see `SECURITY.md` for the support matrix)
- Third-party integrations not officially documented
